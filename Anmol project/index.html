Jammu's Defining Moment: Witness the Power of Ideas at Conclave Badalta JK!

A Vision for Change

The "Conclave Badalta JK" is more than just an event; it is a movement that seeks to inspire collective efforts toward a brighter and more prosperous Jammu and Kashmir. Designed to showcase the socio-political, economic, and cultural evolution of the region since its transition into a Union Territory, the conclave centers around the theme of development.
